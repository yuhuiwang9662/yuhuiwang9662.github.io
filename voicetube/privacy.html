Privacy Policy for VoiceTube

Last updated: October 26, 2025

VoiceTube (“we”, “our”, or “us”) provides a voice-based AI chat and video search experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, retain, and protect information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve our services.

a. Voice and Audio Data

When you speak to the app, your voice input is processed to generate AI responses or to perform actions (for example, searching or playing YouTube videos). Voice data may be temporarily sent to third-party cloud services (such as speech-to-text or AI model providers) to transcribe or generate responses. We do not permanently store raw voice recordings unless you explicitly opt in and are informed otherwise.

b. Usage Data

We may collect anonymous or aggregated usage data such as feature usage, device type, language settings, app crashes, timestamps, and other diagnostics to improve performance and reliability.

c. Account and Preferences

If you create an account or save preferences (for example, selected AI character or voice settings), that data is stored and linked to your user identifier.

d. YouTube API Services

VoiceTube uses YouTube API Services to search and play videos. Requests and interactions with YouTube are subject to Google’s policies and Google’s Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process voice input and generate AI responses;
  • Search for and play YouTube videos based on voice commands;
  • Improve the accuracy and quality of AI interactions;
  • Maintain, secure, and optimize the app experience.

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Retention

Temporary voice and text data may be retained for a short period for processing, quality improvement, or diagnostics. We delete or anonymize voice data after processing unless retention is legally required or you explicitly consent to longer storage.

4. Data Sharing

We may share limited data with trusted service providers that help us operate the app (for example, cloud hosts, AI model providers, analytics providers). All service providers are contractually restricted from using data for other purposes.

We do not share or sell user data to advertisers or unrelated third parties.

5. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure; we encourage safe usage practices and strong account passwords where applicable.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. To exercise these rights, please contact us (see Contact section below).

7. Children’s Privacy

VoiceTube is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or maintain information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. GDPR and EU Users

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the GDPR. The data controller for your personal data is VoiceTube. When required by law, we will rely on your consent to process voice data or other personal data; you may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us. For specific requests (access, portability, deletion), please contact support.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
